# Telegram Storefront — Founding Client Offer

Launch a branded Telegram storefront in five business days, using a live and tested Mini App
as the starting point.

## Founding client pilot — $450

Payment is accepted in **TON or USDT**. The invoice fixes the asset, network, receiving
address, and amount before payment.

### Included

- One branded Telegram Mini App storefront.
- Up to 20 products supplied by the client.
- Client logo, colors, copy, prices, and product imagery.
- Catalog, product, cart, checkout, and order-status screens.
- Telegram bot launch link and outside-Telegram fallback.
- Deployment to an agreed static host.
- Mobile QA on the agreed Telegram clients.
- One revision round after the first working demo.
- Setup and handoff notes.

### Timeline

Five business days, starting after the initial payment and all required assets are received.
Client feedback that arrives late moves the delivery date by the same number of business days.

### Payment schedule

- 50% before work begins.
- 50% after demo approval and before source-code and account handoff.
- A small test transfer may be requested before the first payment.
- The client pays network fees.

Detailed payment terms are provided privately with the statement of work and invoice.

### Not included

- Production payment verification or server-side order authority.
- Telegram `initData` validation.
- Admin dashboard, inventory system, fulfillment, or analytics.
- Mainnet payment activation, fiat payment providers, or Telegram Stars.
- Product photography, product copywriting, or ongoing content entry.
- Hosting, third-party service, blockchain, or exchange fees.
- Ongoing support after the handoff period.

## Production phase — from $1,200

After the pilot is approved, a separate production phase can add backend order authority,
server-side Telegram validation, trusted payment verification, database persistence, admin
tools, and fulfillment integrations. The exact scope, price, and timeline are quoted after a
technical discovery call.

## What the client provides

- Brand assets and approved copy.
- A product list with names, descriptions, prices, and images.
- Telegram bot access or a team member who can make BotFather changes.
- Hosting or domain access when the client wants to own the deployment.
- One decision-maker who can approve the demo and revision.

## How to start

Send the product catalog, brand assets, Telegram bot status, target launch date, and preferred
payment asset. You will receive a fixed-scope statement of work and crypto invoice before any
work begins.
